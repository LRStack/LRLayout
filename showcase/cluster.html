Breaking one item out of the group

Container alignment (--items / --justify) moves every child together. To let a single item escape that, mark the child: .lr-self-* overrides the cross-axis alignment for one item, and .lr-push-* shoves one item (and everything after it) to an edge with an auto-margin.

.lr-push-end  — a trailing item slides to the far edge
