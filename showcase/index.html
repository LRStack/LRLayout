The primitives

cluster

Even-gapped items that wrap. The go-to for most rows.

switcher

A few equal items that flip to a column all at once.

stack

Vertical rhythm between stacked children.

split

Two (or more) zones pushed to the edges.

frame

Fixed aspect-ratio media, cropped to fit.

flank

A sidebar beside a flexible main region.

grid

Auto-fitting responsive columns.

center

A max-width content column, centered.

cover

A viewport-filling region with a centered child.

overlay

A child floated over the rest, centered or pinned.
